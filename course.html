Course Overview

This course is perfect for beginners who want to start their journey into Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Computer Vision with confidence. You don't need to be an expert; we start from the basics and guide you step by step toward advanced ideas in a simple and friendly way.

You will discover how computers learn from data and how AI can understand images and videos. With hands-on projects, real-world examples, and practical exercises, you will see your skills grow with every lesson. By the end of this course, you will be able to build your own models, explore and conduct research, and deploy real AI solutions. This course is your first strong step toward a future in AI.
